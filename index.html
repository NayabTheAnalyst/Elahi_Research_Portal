Research Overview

This section details the primary objectives and contextual background of the study, highlighting why social capital is a critical asset in temporary project organizations.

The Resilience Pathway

In the volatile telecommunications sector of Pakistan, projects often face extreme pressures. This study explores how Project Internal Social Capital (PISC)—the relational and cognitive bonds within a team—allows individuals to maintain performance. The thesis demonstrates that this is not a simple direct link, but a complex serial mediation involving individual psychological strength and altruistic behaviors.
